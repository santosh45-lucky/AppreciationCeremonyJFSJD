Appreciation Ceremony of
JFSJD-CUTTACK

Dear Generations India and Edujobs,

We express our heartfelt gratitude for the incredible opportunity you have provided to students like us to enhance our soft skills and hard skills through your online platform. The resources you offer, such as Canva and Codeacademy, have been invaluable in expanding our knowledge and abilities. Moreover, we are amazed by your commitment to offering online teaching with expert trainers at no cost. This initiative has truly revolutionized the way education is accessed and has opened doors for individuals who may not have had the means to afford courses at traditional institutes. It not only provides a wealth of educational resources but also fosters a supportive and inclusive community of learners. We have been able to connect with fellow students, exchange ideas, and collaborate on various projects, all thanks to the platform you have provided. We are incredibly grateful for the opportunities and experiences we have gained through Generations India and Edujobs. Your commitment to democratizing education and making it accessible to all is truly commendable. Please accept our deepest appreciation for your tireless efforts and dedication. Thank you once again for providing such an amazing opportunity for students like us. I am truly grateful for the invaluable skills we have acquired through your platform. With sincere appreciation,
